About Us

"Take It Cheesy" is a renowned restaurant that has been tantalizing taste buds since its inception in 2008. Nestled in the heart of a bustling city, this culinary gem has consistently delivered an exceptional dining experience for over a decade. Founded by passionate food enthusiasts, the restaurant was envisioned as a haven for cheese lovers. Its warm and inviting ambiance, complete with rustic d√©cor and dimmed lighting, creates the perfect setting for a cozy meal. From couples celebrating anniversaries to families sharing quality time, Take It Cheesy caters to a diverse clientele.
The menu at Take It Cheesy is nothing short of a cheese lover's dream come true. Their signature dishes include delectable cheese fondues, creamy macaroni and cheese, and mouthwatering grilled cheese sandwiches. Each dish is prepared with utmost care and features a selection of high-quality cheeses from around the world.
What truly sets Take It Cheesy apart is its commitment to sourcing local and organic ingredients. From artisanal cheeses to farm-fresh produce, the restaurant prioritizes sustainability and supports local farmers and producers. The skilled chefs at Take It Cheesy continually experiment with unique cheese-infused creations, ensuring that the menu stays fresh and exciting. Seasonal specials, such as truffle-infused cheese dishes in winter and fresh fruit-infused cheese plates in summer, keep patrons eagerly returning for more. The restaurant's exceptional service further enhances the dining experience. The staff is not only attentive but also well-versed in the nuances of cheese, readily offering recommendations and pairings to enhance every customer's visit. For wine and beer enthusiasts, Take It Cheesy boasts an impressive selection of beverages that perfectly complement their cheese-centric dishes. The expertly curated drink menu includes local craft beers, fine wines, and a range of non-alcoholic options.
Over the years, Take It Cheesy has garnered numerous awards and accolades for its dedication to culinary excellence. It has become a go-to destination for cheese aficionados and food critics alike, earning a reputation as one of the city's must-visit dining establishments. In addition to its dine-in experience, Take It Cheesy also offers catering services, making it the ideal choice for events, parties, and gatherings. The restaurant's catering menu features a wide array of cheese platters, fondues, and other crowd-pleasing delights. Beyond its culinary prowess, Take It Cheesy has also demonstrated a strong commitment to community engagement. The restaurant regularly sponsors cheese-tasting events, fundraisers, and educational workshops to share their passion for cheese with the local community.
As Take It Cheesy celebrates its 15th anniversary, it continues to evolve and innovate, solidifying its status as a beloved culinary institution in the city. Whether you're a cheese connoisseur or simply looking for a memorable dining experience, Take It Cheesy remains the ultimate destination for indulging in the cheesy pleasures of life.
